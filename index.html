I'm a Senior DevOps Engineer based in Bangalore with 14 years of engineering experience and 6.5+ years focused on cloud infrastructure, automation, and platform reliability.

My work spans everything from writing Terraform modules that provision entire environments in minutes, to designing security architectures that pass compliance audits.
