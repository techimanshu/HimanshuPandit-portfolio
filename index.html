I'm a Senior DevOps Engineer based in Bangalore with 14 years of engineering experience and 6.5+ years focused on cloud infrastructure, automation, and platform reliability.

At Adamas Tech Consulting, I architect and operate AWS infrastructure supporting edtech platforms serving hundreds of thousands of learners. My work spans everything from writing Terraform modules that provision entire environments in minutes, to designing security architectures that pass compliance audits.

I believe that good infrastructure should be invisible — automated, self-healing, and cost-efficient. When something does go wrong, I want runbooks and observability in place so the fix is fast and the same problem never happens twice.
